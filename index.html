Community First

Better Together

When you hire Breelby Property Care, your money does more than maintain your lawn — it goes back into the neighborhood you live in.

We donate 2% of all proceeds per school zone to help those in need in that zone. And for every 10 lawns mowed or driveways plowed in a school zone, we provide one free like-service to a neighbor in need.
